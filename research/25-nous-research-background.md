## Organization

Nous Research emerged in 2022 as an open-source collective across Discord and Twitter, formalizing in 2023. Prioritizes "user-controlled AI" and decentralization.

## Development Arc Leading to Hermes Agent

1. **Hermes Models (2023-2024):** Open-weight LLM family, highly popular on Ollama and HuggingFace. Known for high steerability and function calling.
2. **Hermes-Function-Calling (Feb 2024):** Function calling training data and examples (1,249 stars)
3. **Hermes 4 (Aug 2025):** Hybrid reasoning with large-scale synthetic data generation. MarkTechPost coverage.
4. **Atropos (Apr 2025):** RL training environments framework for reasoning trajectories (997 stars)
5. **Hermes Agent (Feb 2026):** The full agent framework. Combines Hermes model family strengths with agentic infrastructure.
6. **hermes-agent-self-evolution (Mar 2026):** Evolutionary self-improvement using DSPy + GEPA
7. **hermes-paperclip-adapter (Mar 2026):** Paperclip company integration
8. **autonovel (Mar 2026):** Autonomous novel writing demo/showcase
